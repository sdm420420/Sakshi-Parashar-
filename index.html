About Sakshi

Sakshi Parashar is a talented and graceful Professional Anchor and Singer from Patna, Bihar, known for her charming voice, confidence, and natural ability to connect with audiences.

Whether it's a grand wedding celebration, corporate event, or cultural program — Sakshi lights up the stage with her energy and elegance. Her performances are a perfect blend of professionalism and warmth.

She believes that every event is not just a show — it's an emotion, a memory, and a story. Her presence adds magic and a romantic charm that keeps every guest smiling and engaged till the end.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm available year-round for events across Patna and nearby areas. To book me, simply fill out the booking form or send me a WhatsApp message with your event details. I'll get back to you within 24 hours with availability and pricing information. Early bookings (3-6 months in advance) are preferred for major events.

I specialize in Bollywood songs, devotional music, light classical, and contemporary pop music. I can also perform personalized song requests based on your event's theme and audience preferences. If you have specific song requests, please let me know during the booking process!

Yes, I do travel to nearby cities and towns around Patna and Bihar for events. Travel costs may vary depending on the distance. Please mention your event location when you inquire, and I'll provide you with complete pricing including travel arrangements.

Pricing varies based on the type of event, duration, and services required (anchoring only, singing only, or both). Wedding receptions typically have different rates than corporate events. For accurate pricing, please share your event details through the booking form, and I'll send you a customized quote within 24 hours.

For major events like weddings, I recommend booking 3-6 months in advance to ensure availability. However, I do accommodate last-minute bookings subject to my schedule. For urgent requirements, please contact me directly via WhatsApp or phone call for immediate availability confirmation.

If your event is postponed, I'll try to reschedule on a date that works for both of us based on my availability. For cancellations, please refer to the terms and conditions discussed at the time of booking. I'm flexible and work with clients to find the best solutions.

I come with professional audio equipment including a quality microphone and wireless setup. However, if your venue has its own sound system, I can coordinate with your technical team for seamless integration. We'll confirm all technical details before the event.
